===== MANAGED PLACEMENTS =====

Google may show your ads on additional websites automatically.
You can choose:

AUTOMATIC PLACEMENTS (Recommended to start):
○ Let Google find placements for you
  - Easier, less management
  - Google optimizes based on performance
  - Can exclude poor performers later

MANUAL PLACEMENTS ONLY:
○ Only show on placements you select
  - More control
  - May limit reach
  - More time-intensive


RECOMMENDATION: Start with AUTOMATIC + add high-value placements above.
Then EXCLUDE poor performers after 2 weeks of data.

===== MONITORING & OPTIMIZATION =====

After 2 weeks, review Placements Report:

CHECK:
□ Which placements got impressions
□ Click-through rates by placement
□ Conversions by placement
□ Cost per conversion by placement

OPTIMIZE:
✓ Increase bids on high-performing placements by 20-50%
✓ Exclude placements with >500 impressions and 0 clicks
✓ Exclude placements with clicks but 0 conversions (after sufficient data)
✓ Add similar placements to winners

SUCCESS INDICATORS:
✓ CTR > 1% on Display (2%+ excellent)
✓ Conversion rate > 0.5%
✓ Time on site > 2 minutes
✓ Bounce rate < 60%
